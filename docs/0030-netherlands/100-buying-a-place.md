## Overview

This is the process of buying a place in the Netherlands:

1. **Consult with a mortgage advisor**
1. **Find a place**
1. **Make an offer**
1. **Sign the purchase agreement**
1. **Finalize the mortgage**
1. **Transfer the ownership**
